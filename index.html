Harvest

Stories on Steroids

Let’s talk about you. Because that’s what we’re all about: we make sure people talk about you. A lot of people. Everywhere: online. In the press. On social media. Face to face.

At Harvest, we believe everybody has a story to tell. We help you find that story. We look for a story that is so interesting people will not only listen to it, but engage with it.

We then infuse your story with the steroids it needs to make it travel. A mix of PR pepper, Social salt, Owned and Earned Media ingredients to make it fly.

Harvest tells your story and manages your reputation and conversations. 24/7, on- and offline.

Services

Thinking it
Through

This is where we burn most of our brain grease. First, we search for your Unique Story Proposition. It’s usually found somewhere between your brand guidelines and your engaging content. Secondly, we look for fertile ground: places where people are interested in your story. Places where your story grows bigger.

Listen and
Learn

We rarely make assumptions. We know stuff. We monitor media, measure impressions, analyse data. This way, we can predict trends, identify influencers, and adjust our campaigns in realtime.

Because your story deserves better than an educated guess.

Talking
the Walk

Here we put our money where our mouth is. We implement our strategy and create the actual content. Be it press releases, conversation management or native advertising.​​

This is where you touch people with your story.
